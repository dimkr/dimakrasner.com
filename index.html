dimakrasner.com

Hi! I'm Dima Krasner, a FOSS hacker from the Holy Land.

I specialize in C, heavy debugging, R&D, scripting, hacking, automation, porting and distro development. I develop small, tight solutions that nail down the problem elegantly. Also, I'm a trumpetist (mostly lead) and a philosophy student.

Currently, I work at Hexadite, but always looking for new adventures.

You can reach me at dima@this site (unless you're a spambot) or via GitHub, LinkedIn or NotABug.

Most of my work is done in my spare time; donations are always welcome and appreciated!
